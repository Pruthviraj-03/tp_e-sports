About Turning Point E-sports Organization

Private Limited

Turning Point Esports is an Indian Esports Organization. It was formed in 2018 just after the first official pubg tournament was announced.It has an interesting story behind its name. There was a clan in mini militia and clash of clan with the name Turning Point and when the game of pubg was launched, the members started playing under the same name TP. Team TURNING POINT BGMI, owner, origin, roster, achievements, history, net worth.

The owner of Turning Point Esports is Sagar Shinde aka Lassi Bhai. He is a famous streamer and BGMI player. He was also a part of the first roster which entered a tournament as he himself was a PUBG player. The current lineup of Turning Point is considered one of the best in India and is recognised as a competition by the leading teams in global tournaments. It is the most experienced indian when it comes to global tournaments. Most of the teams try to avoid taking fights with the team as the team is fearless and confident and have the skills and synergy to back their aggression. A lot of players have been part of team Turning Point. Among them the most successful and impactful lineup are “AXIOS”, “BRIAN”, “NODDY”, "DADA".

DADA is the current IGL for the team. He is known for his controlled aggression, under his leadership the team performs magnificently and he is calm even in pressure situations. He came to the spotlight when he played for team OR in a tournament and their performance improved significantly. AXIOS and BRIAN are the main fraggers for the team. While NODDY is considered the most valuable support player in the country.

T urning Point

Turning Point Esports is an Indian Esports organization founded by Pruthviraj Kurane, aka Axios. They are one of the leading Esports organizations in India, hosting a variety of professional Esports teams.

They have teams in three games and a slew of brilliant content creators, with plans to rapidly expand in the near future. They are on a quest to become a premium gaming/lifestyle brand.

Their objective is to create tomorrow's stars, build a strong community, and put India on the gaming world map.
